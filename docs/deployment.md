# Deployment Phases

## Phase 1 — Discovery

During this stage we identify:

- Business goals
- Customer journey
- Existing systems
- Communication channels
- Automation opportunities

Deliverables:

- Business assessment
- AI strategy
- Integration plan

---

## Phase 2 — Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base is prepared using company-specific information.

Typical sources include:

- Website
- Company documents
- Services
- Products
- Policies
- FAQs
- Internal procedures

---

## Phase 3 — AI Configuration

The AI Engine is configured to match business requirements.

Typical configuration includes:

- Conversation behavior
- Tone of voice
- Languages
- Business rules
- Escalation logic
- Customer qualification

---

## Phase 4 — Integration

The platform is connected to business systems.

Typical integrations include:

- Website
- WhatsApp Business
- CRM
- Booking Systems
- Email
- Internal applications

---

## Phase 5 — Testing

Every deployment is validated before production.

Testing includes:

- AI responses
- Integrations
- Security
- Automation
- Performance
- User acceptance

---

## Phase 6 — Production

The platform is deployed into the production environment.

Objectives include:

- High availability
- Stable performance
- Secure operation
- Continuous monitoring

# Configuration Checklist

Before deployment ensure:

- Business information reviewed
- Knowledge Base completed
- AI behavior approved
- CRM connected
- Website integrated
- WhatsApp configured
- Security reviewed
- Testing completed

---

# Go-Live Checklist

Recommended checklist:

- AI verified
- Integrations verified
- Notifications enabled
- Monitoring active
- Backup strategy confirmed
- Documentation completed
