# 🔒 SamChe AI Platform Security

Security is a fundamental principle of the SamChe AI Platform.

Every component has been designed to support enterprise-grade protection, secure communication, controlled access, and responsible handling of business information.

---

# Security Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence should not only be intelligent—it must also be secure.

The SamChe AI Platform follows a security-first approach throughout the entire platform lifecycle, from authentication and communication to data protection and infrastructure.

Our objectives are:

- Protect customer information
- Secure AI interactions
- Prevent unauthorized access
- Ensure business continuity
- Support enterprise deployment

# Incident Response

If a security issue is identified, recommended actions include:

1. Identify the issue.
2. Restrict affected access.
3. Preserve operational logs.
4. Investigate root cause.
5. Apply corrective actions.
6. Verify platform stability.
