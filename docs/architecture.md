# 🏗 SamChe AI Platform Architecture

The SamChe AI Platform follows a modular, scalable, and enterprise-ready architecture designed to automate customer communication, streamline business workflows, and integrate seamlessly with existing business systems.

# Core Components

The platform is composed of independent modules that communicate through a centralized AI layer.

## AI Engine

The AI Engine is responsible for:

- Understanding customer intent
- Generating intelligent responses
- Context management
- Conversation memory
- Workflow triggering

---

## Website AI Chatbot

The website chatbot enables businesses to communicate with visitors in real time.

Capabilities include:

- Lead generation
- Customer support
- FAQ automation
- Appointment booking
- Product recommendations

---

## WhatsApp AI Assistant

The WhatsApp module allows organizations to automate conversations using AI.

Features include:

- Instant replies
- Lead qualification
- PDF understanding
- Voice message processing
- Business consultation

---

## Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base stores organization-specific information used by AI.

Examples include:

- Company services
- Internal documentation
- Product catalogs
- Policies
- Frequently Asked Questions

---

## CRM Integration

The CRM layer synchronizes customer information with AI conversations.

Supported processes:

- Lead synchronization
- Customer history
- Sales tracking
- Contact management

---

## Workflow Automation

Business automation allows repetitive operations to be executed automatically.

Examples:

- Follow-up messages
- Internal notifications
- Appointment reminders
- Customer onboarding

---

## Analytics

The analytics engine provides business insights including:

- Customer interactions
- AI performance
- Lead statistics
- Conversation quality
- Automation reports
